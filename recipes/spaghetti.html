Spaghetti & Meat Sauce

Description

This is a simple recipe ideal for beginner level cooks

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles (1:1 noodle/meat ratio)
  • Ground meat (Turkey in this instance)
  • Cut vegetables of choice (Chopped spinach in this instance)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Lightly oil medium-sized pot and preheat on medium heat
  2. Add in chopped spinach to the pot
  3. Add in ground turkey to sizzling pot, break it up
  4. Season turkey generously, cook until thoroughly browned
  5. Add in and stir tomato sauce
  6. Simmer meat sauce for 20 minutes
  7. In a separate pot bring water to a boil
  8. Cook spaghetti in boiling water for 11 minutes
  9. Drain water
  10. Enjoy
