Gym Bro Oatmeal Recipe

Description

Oatmeal, but for making gains

Ingredients

  • Quakers Oats oatmeal
  • Milk
  • Peanut butter
  • Granola
  • Cinnamon
  • Fruit of choice

Steps

  1. Follow packaging instructions for cooking oatmeal
  2. Add a few table spoons of peanut butter
  3. Season with cinnamon
  4. Sprinkle some granola
  5. Top with fruit of choice
  6. Enjoy
